A life of seasonal employment and other misadventures of my 30s

Just some fun photos and stories from working and traveling in my 30s

St. Louis, MO

After being born and raised in St. Louis, MO, I decided to leave at 30 and travel. I returned in 2018 and had a great time running food and beverage services for multiple large corporate and public events.

Southern Road Trip

In October of 2019, prior to relocating to Kansas City, my patner and I took a 2 week road trip through the Southern US including stops in Austin, the Texas gulf coast, and New Orleans.

Mat-Su Valley, Alaska

I spent the two Summers working at the NOLS Alaska branch in Palmer, Alaska. NOLS is a nonprofit global wilderness school whose mission is to be the leading source and teacher of wilderness skills and leadership that serve people and the environment.

San Antonio

I had the opportunity to spend six months in San Antonio, TX working in the oldest bar on the city's historic riverfront.
